📜 Poetry

🌙 Tears of the Moon

By Hassan Jatoi

My heart, a broken violin of time,
Plays love’s last tune in rhyme sublime.

The moon weeps soft on my window’s glass,
Reciting verses of what did pass.

Her voice, once sunlight, now fades in gloom,
Leaving perfume in sorrow’s room.

I breathe her name in the wind’s cold sigh,
And stars reply with a mournful cry.

Love died, yet its ghost forever croons —
In my chest, the Tears of the Moon.

🌹 The Grave of Roses

By Hassan Jatoi

Beneath my tears, the roses sleep,
Their fragrance buried, memories deep.

Each petal sighs with silent pain,
For love once lost shall bloom again.

Thy name still burns upon the breeze,
Like whispered prayers through dying trees.

The soil drinks grief where passion grows,
And thorns adorn the grave of rose.

So let the night in mourning close —
My heart remains The Grave of Roses.

🌑 Thy Shadow Lingers

By Hassan Jatoi

Thy shadow walks where thou art gone,
It haunts my nights, it mocks the dawn.

Thy whisper drifts through empty air,
A ghostly hymn of lost despair.

The walls recall thy gentle tread,
Though time proclaims our love is dead.

Yet every dusk, my spirit sings,
For still, Thy Shadow Lingers.

💌 Letters Unsent

By Hassan Jatoi

The ink of my pain stains the sky,
Where dreams of thee in silence die.

I wrote thy name with trembling hands,
On paper drenched by time’s demands.

The words, unspoken, fade away,
Like ghosts of love that could not stay.

O heart, thou keep’st what fate hath rent —
My soul is full of Letters Unsent.

💋 A Kiss Never Given

By Hassan Jatoi

Thy lips were dawn, yet night they stayed,
My heart their promise disobeyed.

A single breath, so close, so near,
Yet fate stood still — love drowned in fear.

The wind did ache to see us part,
Two trembling souls, one shattered heart.

I spoke no word, nor didst thou move,
Silence became the grave of love.

The stars beheld our yearning crime,
Two hearts that froze in endless time.

Thy perfume lingers on my pain,
As echoes fall like tender rain.

No kiss to bless, no vow to keep,
Just dreams that burn when angels sleep.

And thus, beneath the weeping heaven,
I mourn A Kiss Never Given.

⚰️ The Poet’s Coffin

By Hassan Jatoi

Each verse I write becomes my wound,
In tears and ink, my soul is bound.

My quill now weeps, my candle dies,
As grief takes flight through midnight skies.

The words once kissed by beauty’s grace,
Now carve thy name on time’s cold face.

The paper shivers, drenched in pain,
It feels thy loss in every stain.

No muse remains to light my art,
Just echoes sob within my heart.

Each rhyme, a nail in dreams I built,
Each sigh, the shroud of tender guilt.

The poet sleeps where sorrows sing,
His coffin lined with love’s broken string.

Beneath the moon, my fate is written —
Here lies The Poet’s Coffin.

🕯️ Whispers Beneath the Ruins

By Hassan Jatoi

The moon remembers the ache of our sigh,
It weeps for the nights that refused to die.

Your name still burns on the edge of pain,
A rose in ashes, beneath the rain.

Dreams I buried beneath my chest,
Still knock at the door of my broken rest.

The stars fell quiet when you turned away,
And time forgot the words to say.

Every breath I take, a wound untold,
In silence, my heart turns pale and cold.

Your shadow walks through my empty hall,
A ghost of love that haunts it all.

If love was a sin, then I am its crime,
Condemned to remember you through time.

I kiss your memory, though it bleeds,
For sorrow is the garden where love still feeds.

💔 Tears of the Forgotten

By Hassan Jatoi

I wrote your name on the river’s skin,
It vanished soft, as sins begin.

Your laughter lingers, cruel and kind,
A mirror cracked within my mind.

My heart’s cathedral burns with shame,
Each prayer I whisper bears your name.

Loneliness wears your voice tonight,
In every silence, I lose the light.

The world moves on, I stand alone,
A kingdom of dust, a heart of stone.

The wind recites our final song,
Where love was right—but fate was wrong.

My tears have learned the art of flight,
They fall like stars into endless night.

O grief, my crown, my faithful friend,
You’ll walk beside me till the end.

🔥 Elegy of the Broken Flame

By Hassan Jatoi

Once we were fire, now only smoke,
Love’s fragile glass forever broke.

You left your scent on my weary soul,
A wound too deep to make me whole.

My dreams have turned to haunted seas,
Where echoes whisper your decrees.

The candles mourn with shivering breath,
Their light consumed by living death.

I speak to the moon, she turns away,
She too has known a love’s decay.

Each heartbeat begs for your return,
Yet finds the silence—cold, stern.

If heaven could weep, it would tonight,
For love that died without its right.

O memory, my sweetest chain,
Bind me forever to my pain.
